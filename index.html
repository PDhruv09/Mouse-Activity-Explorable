Mouse Activity & Temperature - Explorable Explanations

Analyzing the daily activity and temperature variations of mice over a 14-day period.

What is being visualized?

This project presents interactive visualizations to analyze core body temperature and activity levels of mice, considering daily rhythms and the estrus cycle.

Why is it being visualized?

The aim is to explore how activity levels and temperature fluctuate across time, between genders, and during the estrus cycle.

How is it being visualized?

Using D3.js, we visualize time-series trends, scatter plots, and heatmaps to illustrate meaningful patterns in the dataset.
