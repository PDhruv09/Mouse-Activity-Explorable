Estrus Cycle Impact on Mouse Physiology

Analyzing how the estrus cycle affects temperature and activity levels in female mice.

What is being visualized?

A scatter plot comparing temperature levels during estrus and non-estrus periods.

Why is it being visualized?

To determine how ovulation affects body temperature and activity levels in female mice.
