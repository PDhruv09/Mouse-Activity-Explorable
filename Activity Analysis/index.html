Mouse Activity Analysis

Exploring how mice activity levels fluctuate throughout the day.

What is being visualized?

A scatter plot representing the activity levels of mice at different times of the day.

Why is it being visualized?

To understand daily patterns in mouse behavior, particularly differences in activity during light and dark periods.

How is it being visualized?

The scatter plot below maps activity levels against time of day, using D3.js for interactivity.
